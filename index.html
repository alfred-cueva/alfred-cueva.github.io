Alfred Cueva

I'm a robotics engineer at Samsung C&T where I work on system integration for collaborative robots .

I received my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from Seoul National University during which I interned as a research intern at Dynamic Robotics System Lab, advised by Prof. Jaehung Park and Dr. Daegyu Lim focusing on Reinforcement Learning and Control for legged locomotion. I also worked as a Research Intern at Soft Robotics & Bionics Lab under the guidance of Prof. Yong-Lae Park on soft robotic sensing for industrial robots. Apart from this I spent some time at Beam Studio developing generative models for live video editing.
