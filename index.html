Alfred Cueva

I'm a Robotics Engineer at Samsung C&T where I work on system integration for collaborative robots .

I received my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from Seoul National University during which I was a research intern at Dynamic Robotics System Lab, advised by Prof. Jaehung Park and Dr. Daegyu Lim focusing on Reinforcement Learning and Control for legged locomotion. I also worked as a Research Intern at Soft Robotics & Bionics Lab under the guidance of Prof. Yong-Lae Park on soft robotic sensing for industrial robots. Apart from this I spent some time at Beam Studio developing generative models for live video editing.

Research

I am interested in Learning-Based Control, Reinforcement Learning and Motion planning with specific focus on legged robotics. I would also like to explore legged loco-manipulation and intelligent robotic manipulation

RL-Policy Guided Optimal Design of Parallel Elastic Actuator for Weak Actuation of Bipedal Robot
Alfred Cueva, Jaehung Park, Yonglae Park
Bachelor's Thesis / Outstanding BS Thesis Presentation Award

We design an optimization framework using model-free meta reinforcement learning, and its application to optimizing kinematics and actuator parameters of quadrupedal robots with weak actuation.

Sungjae Park*, Hyelim Choi*, Taekyun Kim*
Graduate Course Project (Probabilistic Graphical Models, Spring 2022)
